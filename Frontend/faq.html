Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view my electricity bill?

You can view your electricity bill by logging into your account and navigating to the 'Billing' section, where all your recent bills are listed.

How do I pay my bill?

To pay your bill, simply visit the 'Pay Bill' section in your account, select the bill you want to pay, and choose your preferred payment method.

What should I do if I think my bill is incorrect?

If you believe your bill is incorrect, please file a dispute via 'Help and Support' section in Settings menu or email us at support@electricitybilling.com for further assistance.

How can I update my contact information?

To update your contact details, go to the 'Account Settings' section, and you can edit your phone number, email, and address there.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to receive a password reset email.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support by visiting the 'Help' section, or by emailing us at support@electricitybilling.com.
