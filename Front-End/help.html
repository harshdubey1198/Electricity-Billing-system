Help & Support

FAQs

  • How do I register a new account?

    To register a new account, navigate to the Sign Up page, fill in the necessary details, and click the submit button. You will receive a confirmation email once registration is complete.

  • What if I forget my password?

    If you forget your password, click the 'Forgot Password' link on the Login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

  • How do I view my current bill?

    After logging in, navigate to the 'My Bills' section under your profile to view your current and past bills.

  • How can I pay my bill?

    You can pay your bill online using credit/debit card or net banking by visiting the 'Pay Bill' section after logging into your account.
